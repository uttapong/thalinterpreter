Thalassemia Interpreter

A Thalassemia interpretation software built on a robust rules. Thalassemia Interpreter made it easy for doctors,medical technologists or any members to process and interprete their personality trait from Hemoglobin typing profile. It can also help couples to predict the risk and prevent the inherited babies.
